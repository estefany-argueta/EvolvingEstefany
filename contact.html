What to expect

I aim to respond to emails within 1–2 business days during the academic year. If you're a student interested in research opportunities, mentorship, or outreach collaboration, I'd be happy to chat. For collaborations or inquiries, please include a brief description of your interest or project.

Research & Mentoring Inquiries

If you're interested in:

  • Undergraduate or graduate research — I take on motivated students interested in thermal biology, climate adaptation, and physiological ecology.
  • Postdoctoral collaboration — I'm interested in joint proposals and interdisciplinary work bridging physiology, ecology, and genomics.
  • Field or lab partnerships — Reach out about sea turtle research, fish physiology, or other ectotherm-focused projects.
  • Community science or outreach — I engage in K-12 and community teaching events; let's talk about collaboration!
